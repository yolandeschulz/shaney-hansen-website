5. Photography bookings, image consent and children

Photography work may involve images of individuals, families, children, schools, pre-schools, teams, events and private moments.

Images will only be used for portfolio, website, social media, advertising or marketing purposes where permission has been given, where the use is covered by a booking agreement, or where another lawful basis applies.

Special care will be taken when images include children, school groups or private events.

Parents, guardians, schools, organisations or clients may request that certain images not be used publicly. These requests should be made in writing.

Final image usage, portfolio permission and social media permission should be confirmed in the booking agreement or written client approval.
