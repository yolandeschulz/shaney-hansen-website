## 2. What we collect and why

When you contact us through the website, by email, WhatsApp or social media, we may collect your name, contact details, business or brand name, social handles, preferred date, shoot or event location, enquiry type, and any details you share about your project. If analytics is switched on, we may also collect basic, anonymous website-usage information.

We only ask for what we reasonably need to respond to you, prepare a quote, manage a booking, deliver your work or provide social media support. We do not sell your personal information.

**Contact form:** The website may use a third-party form or email service to deliver your enquiry. You can also contact Shaney directly by email or WhatsApp.

## 3. Photography, image consent and children

Photography may involve images of individuals, families, children, schools, pre-schools, teams and private events. Images are only used for portfolio, website, social media or marketing where permission has been given, where a booking agreement covers it, or where another lawful basis applies. Extra care is taken with images of children, school groups and private events.

Parents, guardians, schools, organisations or clients may ask, in writing, that specific images not be used publicly. Final image and portfolio permissions should be confirmed in the booking agreement or written client approval.

For social media management clients, brand content and account access are handled securely, used only for the agreed service, and never shared publicly unless approved.
