bio: >
  Hi, I'm Shaney.


  I'm a photographer and visual storyteller based in Lichtenburg, in South
  Africa's North West — though my favourite sessions have taken me well beyond
  it.

  Photography, for me, has never been about stiff poses. It's the way a bride
  laughs walking out of the chapel, a toddler's first two-fisted grab at their
  birthday cake, the quiet minute a new mom doesn't realise she's being
  photographed. I work slowly and calmly, mostly in natural light, and I'd
  rather wait for a real moment than manufacture one.


  Since 2016, my camera has taken me across the full spread of life's occasions:
  weddings and couples, maternity and newborns, families, cake smashes, school
  portraits and sports days, and corporate and branding work for businesses and
  professional teams. Many of my styled sessions happen on sets I plan and build
  myself — from seasonal concepts to little worlds made for one small person's
  big milestone. Creating the scene is half the joy; photographing what happens
  inside it is the other half.


  That same eye for story is why I also manage social media for selected brands,
  including Team Up Athletics in Katy, Texas — helping businesses show up online
  with the warmth and consistency their work deserves.

  When I'm not behind the camera, I spend time in the kitchen experimenting and
  cooking with my three children. I graduated with a post graduate degree in
  Consumer Scienes from the University of Pretoria before getting married to a
  farmer and starting a family based life on our 2500 acre homestead in South
  Africa.

  If you're planning a wedding, expecting a little one, building a brand, or
  simply want your people photographed the way they really are — I'd love to
  hear your story.
