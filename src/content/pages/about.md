bio: >
  Hi, I'm Shaney.


  I'm a photographer and visual storyteller based in Lichtenburg, in South
  Africa's North West — though my favourite sessions have taken me well beyond
  it, including work in the USA and Singapore.

  Photography, for me, has never been about stiff poses. It's the way a bride
  laughs walking out of the chapel, a toddler's first two-fisted grab at their
  birthday cake, the quiet minute a new mom doesn't realise she's being
  photographed. I work slowly and calmly, mostly in natural light, and I'd
  rather wait for a real moment than manufacture one.


  Over the past decade or so, my camera has taken me across the full spread of
  life's occasions — weddings and couples, maternity and newborns, families,
  cake smashes, school portraits and sports days, and corporate and branding
  work for businesses and professional teams. Many of my styled sessions begin
  with a concept I develop and style myself — a seasonal scene, a brand look, or
  a little world made for one small person's big milestone. Shaping the concept
  and the styling is half the joy; photographing what happens inside it is the
  other half.


  That same eye for story is why I also create and manage social media content
  for selected brands, including Team Up Athletics in Katy, Texas — helping
  businesses show up online with the warmth and consistency their work deserves.
  My photography has also appeared in local published media over the years.

  When I'm not behind the camera, I spend time in the kitchen experimenting and
  cooking with my three children. I graduated with a postgraduate degree in
  Consumer Sciences from the University of Pretoria before getting married to a
  farmer and starting a family-based life on our homestead in South Africa.

  If you're planning a wedding, expecting a little one, building a brand, or
  simply want your people photographed the way they really are — I'd love to
  hear your story.
