# Where to edit what — Pages CMS map

This guide walks through the website page by page, top to bottom, and tells you exactly where to change each thing in Pages CMS (app.pagescms.org). Names in **bold** are the entries in the left-hand menu and the field labels you'll see.

General rule: photos and wording live in the page entries; photos for the galleries live in **🖼️ Portfolio galleries**; contact details and links live in **⚙️ Site settings**. After saving, the change is stored immediately — it appears on the live website after the next deployment.

## Every page (header & footer)

- Round logo top-left → **⚙️ Site settings** → *Logo*
- Phone/WhatsApp, email, location in the footer → **⚙️ Site settings** → *Phone*, *Email*, *Location*
- Footer wording and availability line → **⚙️ Site settings** → *Footer wording*, *Availability line*
- Facebook / Instagram links in the footer → **⚙️ Site settings** → *Facebook page link*, *Instagram link*
- "Read/Leave a Google Review" links → **⚙️ Site settings** → *Google Reviews link*, *Google Review request link* (buttons appear once real links are saved)

## Homepage — 🏠 Home page

- Big photo or video at the very top → *Hero video* (clear it to show the photo) and *Big photo/video poster at the top*
- Big headline on the photo → *Big headline at the top of the homepage*
- Sentence under it and the two buttons → *Sentence under the big headline*, *First button — text*, *Second button — text*
- Welcome section → *Welcome section — heading / text*
- The three service cards (Photography / Creative Direction / Social Media) → *Service card 1/2/3 — title, text, photo*
- Featured gallery cards → **🖼️ Portfolio galleries**: each gallery's *Cover photo*, with *Show on homepage?* ticked (order set by *Display order*)
- Creative direction section (two layered photos) → *Creative direction section — heading, text, large photo, small overlapping photo*
- Social media section → *Social media section — heading, text, photo*
- Destination section (dark, photo background) → *Destination section — heading, text, background photo*
- "From the portfolio" strip of six photos → automatic: the cover photos of the first six galleries (by *Display order*) in **🖼️ Portfolio galleries**
- How it works (four steps) → *'How it works' — heading, intro text, the four steps*
- Kind Words testimonials → **💬 Testimonials** (homepage shows items with *Show on the homepage?* ticked)
- Bottom enquiry block → *Bottom enquiry block — heading, text, button text*

## About page — 👋 About page

- Large photo and small overlapping photo → *Main photo*, *Small overlapping photo*
- Headline, intro, personal bio → *Headline*, *Intro paragraph*, *Personal bio*
- Her style / What to expect / Why choose Shaney → the matching heading and text fields
- Bottom enquiry block → *Bottom enquiry block — heading, text*

## Photography page — 📸 Photography page & services

- Headline, sentence and photo at the top → *Headline at the top of the page*, *Sentence under the headline*, *Photo at the top of the page*
- The two buttons at the top → *First button — text*, *Second button — text*
- Each service (Weddings, Family, Newborn…) → open the service in *The photography services* list: *Who it is for*, *What clients can expect*, *Package / pricing details*, *Photo*
- Testimonials on this page → **💬 Testimonials** (items whose *Which service was this for?* is a photography service)
- Bottom enquiry block → *Bottom enquiry block — heading, text*

## Social media page

Split over two entries:

- **📱 Social media page (top section & photo grid)** → headline, top photo, button texts, the photo grid ("Visuals made to stop the scroll") and the bottom enquiry block
- **📱 Social media services, packages & international section** → the two service cards, "who this is for" and "what Shaney can help with" lists, the four package cards, and the dark international section
- The Team Up Athletics story → **⭐ Social media case studies**
- Testimonials on this page → **💬 Testimonials** (service type: Social Media Management / Content Creation)

## Creative direction page

- **🎨 Creative direction page** → headline, top photo, portfolio proof text, buttons, bottom enquiry block
- **🎨 Creative direction — copy & capabilities list** → the main copy and the capabilities list
- The three proof gallery cards (Styled, Studio, Cake Smash) → **🖼️ Portfolio galleries** (their cover photos)

## Destination page

- **✈️ Destination page** → headline, top photo, quote button, bottom enquiry block
- **✈️ Destination — copy, value section & planning list** → "Why Shaney" copy, international value text, "ideal for" list, planning placeholders

## Portfolio pages — 🖼️ Portfolio galleries

- The category cards on /portfolio → each gallery's *Cover photo*, *Gallery title*, *Short description*
- The photos inside a gallery → open the gallery → *Photos in this gallery* → *Add* (upload the photo, write the description/alt text)
- Order of the categories → *Display order (1 = first)*

## Contact page — 📞 Contact page

- Headline and supporting sentence → *Headline*, *Sentence under the headline*
- Consent tick-box wording → *Consent tick-box wording*
- The enquiry type options in the form are fixed (technical) — ask your developer to change those
- Direct contact details in the sidebar → **⚙️ Site settings**

## Privacy policy page — 🔒 Privacy policy page

- The whole policy text → *Policy text* (currently a placeholder)

## Testimonials — 💬 Testimonials (Kind Words)

To publish one: fill in *The client's words*, *Client name*, *Where the review came from* and *Which service was this for?*, then tick BOTH *Client permission approved?* and *Show on website?*. Tick *Show on the homepage?* for the few best ones. Full guidance: REVIEWS_AND_TESTIMONIALS_GUIDE.md.

## Not editable in Pages CMS (on purpose)

- The menu items in the header and the page addresses (structural)
- Small section labels like "Portfolio", "Contact", "The experience" (design elements)
- The enquiry form's dropdown options (wired to enquiry tracking)
- The favicon (browser tab icon)

For any of these, ask your developer.
